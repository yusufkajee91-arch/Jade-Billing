## Reconciliation fix summary (Phase 6)

The gaps resolved by fixing sign-aware type assignment:
- **Trust**: matter-ledger amounts use DEBTORS perspective. Positive Trust Receipts (+amount) are reversals → `trust_payment`; negative Trust Payments (-amount) are refunds → `trust_receipt`. Applying the sign rule closed the R380k gap.
- **Business**: bank-statement amounts use BANK perspective (+=in, -=out). Negative Business Receipts are refunds → `business_payment`; positive Business Payments are deposits/reversals → `business_receipt`. Also added `Staff Payment` type handling (Reference column absent for payroll rows). Closed the R466k gap.
